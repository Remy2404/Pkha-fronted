Size Guide

Foundation

Find your perfect foundation shade and coverage level with our guide. We'll help you measure your skin tone and undertone to ensure a flawless match.

Coverage Levels

Light

Provides a natural, sheer finish, ideal for everyday wear and minimal imperfections.

Medium

Offers a balanced look, covering minor blemishes and evening out skin tone.

Full

Delivers maximum coverage, concealing significant imperfections and creating a polished look.

How to Measure

1

Prepare Your Skin

Cleanse your face and wait 15 minutes before measuring.

2

Find Natural Light

Use natural light for the most accurate shade matching.

3

Test on Jawline

Apply a small amount of foundation to your jawline and blend.

4

Check for Blend

The shade that disappears is your perfect match.
