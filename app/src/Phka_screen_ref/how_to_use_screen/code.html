Tips & Tricks

Application

Apply a thin layer of the product to clean, dry skin, avoiding the eye area. Leave on for 10-15 minutes, then rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.

schedule

Frequency

For best results, use 2-3 times a week. If you have sensitive skin, start with once a week and gradually increase frequency as tolerated.

water_drop

Moisturizing

Follow with your favorite moisturizer to lock in hydration and keep your skin feeling soft and supple.
