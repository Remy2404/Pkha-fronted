About Us

About Phka

Phka is a beauty brand dedicated to enhancing your natural radiance with high-quality, sustainable products. Our journey began with a passion for clean beauty and a commitment to ethical practices. We believe in the power of nature and science to create effective, luxurious skincare and makeup that you can feel good about using.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Anya Sharma, Phka was born out of a desire to create a beauty brand that aligns with modern values. Anya, a former chemist, envisioned a line of products that are not only effective but also environmentally conscious and cruelty-free. Inspired by her grandmother's traditional beauty remedies, Anya combined ancient wisdom with cutting-edge research to develop Phka's signature formulations.

Mission & Values

At Phka, our mission is to empower individuals to embrace their unique beauty while promoting a healthier planet. We are committed to:

  • Sustainability: Using eco-friendly packaging and responsibly sourced ingredients.
  • Transparency: Providing clear information about our products and practices.
  • Inclusivity: Celebrating diversity and creating products for all skin types and tones.
  • Quality: Ensuring our products meet the highest standards of efficacy and safety.
